Fulltime Goals scored at Home and Away for all teams
Core JS Files

This dot plot shows the number of goals scored at fulltime both at Home and Away for all the teams from 1995-2017. Each team is colour coded and is represented in the Legend on the right hand side. As we can clearly see that the highest goalscorers are Manchester United at the topmost right of the graph with 998 Home goals and 728 Away goals. The tooltip on the graph helps us to view the same. At the e=other extreme its Sheffield United with 13 and 6 goals respectively.
