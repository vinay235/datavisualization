Count of goals scored at Home, Away and Draw
Core JS Files

This line graph represents the total number of goals scored at Home, Away and the Draws from 1995-96 season to 2017-18. The Away, Home and Draw categories are represented in different colours. We can clearly see that te count of Home goals is always greater for every year as the teams are usually more comfortable scoring in their respective home grounds. The Away games follow the trend of goals scored in Draws. A hover tooltip is also provided to check the number of goals scored for the respective categories. The most number of Home goals were scored in 2009-10 season.
