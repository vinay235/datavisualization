Manchester City and Liverpool over the years.

The above line graph shows the goals scored by Manchester City and Liverpool over the years from 1995 to 2018. This graph is executed using Bokeh library from python. We can see there are many options available on the right hand side to interact with the graph such as spanning and zooming. We have selected these two teams as they are the current winner and runners up of 2018/2019 season of EPL.
